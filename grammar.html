📚 English Grammar Course

English 🔹 Course Duration: 1 to 3 Months (Based on Student Level) 🔹 Course Goal: Learn essential grammar rules for clear and confident English speaking. Avoid common mistakes in sentence structure, tense usage, and pronunciation.

🟢 1. Basic Grammar for Beginners

✅ Sentence Structure (Subject + Verb + Object)

✅ Parts of Speech (Noun, Pronoun, Verb, Adjective, Adverb, Preposition, Conjunction, Interjection)

✅ Types of Sentences (Positive, Negative, Interrogative)

✅ Articles (a, an, the) – When & how to use them

✅ Common Prepositions (in, on, at, under, behind, beside)

✅ Basic Tenses – Simple Present, Simple Past, Simple Future

🔹 Example Sentences:

❌ “He go to school.” → ✅ “He goes to school.” ❌ “She is teacher.” → ✅ “She is a teacher.”

🟡 2. Intermediate Grammar for Fluency

✅ Tenses in Detail

✔️ Present Simple vs. Present Continuous ✔️ Past Simple vs. Past Continuous ✔️ Future Simple vs. Future Continuous

✅ Modal Verbs (can, could, should, must, may, might)

✔️ Expressing ability, permission, and obligation

✅ Pronouns & Their Correct Usage (I, me, mine, myself)

✔️ Difference between he/him, she/her, they/them

✅ Adjectives & Adverbs (Difference & Usage)

✔️ "She runs fast (adverb)" vs. "She is fast (adjective)"

✅ Comparatives & Superlatives

✔️ "Bigger vs. Biggest," "More beautiful vs. Most beautiful"

✅ Common Mistakes & How to Fix Them

✔️ “I didn’t went.” → “I didn’t go.” ✔️ “She speak good.” → “She speaks well.”

🔴 3. Advanced Grammar for Mastery

✅ Advanced Tenses

✔️ Present Perfect & Present Perfect Continuous ✔️ Past Perfect & Past Perfect Continuous ✔️ Future Perfect & Future Perfect Continuous

✅ Conditionals (If-Clauses)

✔️ Zero Conditional: "If you heat ice, it melts." ✔️ First Conditional: "If you study, you will pass." ✔️ Second Conditional: "If I were you, I would learn English." ✔️ Third Conditional: "If I had studied, I would have passed."

✅ Direct & Indirect Speech

✔️ Direct: She said, "I am tired." ✔️ Indirect: She said she was tired.

✅ Active vs. Passive Voice

✔️ Active: "She wrote the letter." ✔️ Passive: "The letter was written by her."

✅ Phrasal Verbs & Idioms

✔️ "Give up" = Quit ✔️ "Break down" = Stop working ✔️ "Piece of cake" = Very easy

✅ Business & Professional Grammar

✔️ Writing formal emails & reports ✔️ Using polite language in meetings ✔️ Avoiding slang in professional settings

🔥 Bonus Features

✅ Daily Practice Exercises ✅ Real-Life Conversations (Role-plays & Debates) ✅ Grammar Correction Sessions
