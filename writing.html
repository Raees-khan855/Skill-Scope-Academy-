📚 English Writing Course

🔹 Course Duration: 1 to 3 Months (Based on Student Level)

🔹 Course Goal:

✅ Learn basic to advanced writing skills for daily use, work, and academic purposes.

✅ Improve sentence structure, grammar, and vocabulary in writing.

✅ Develop skills for emails, essays, business writing, and creative writing.

🟢 1. Basic Writing Skills (Beginners)

✅ Sentence Formation

✔️ Simple sentences: “I like to read.” ✔️ Compound sentences: “I like to read, and I enjoy writing.” ✔️ Complex sentences: “When I read books, I learn new things.”

✅ Punctuation & Capitalization

✔️ Using periods (.), commas (,), question marks (?), and exclamation marks (!) correctly. ✔️ Capitalizing the first word in a sentence and proper nouns.

✅ Basic Paragraph Writing

✔️ Writing short paragraphs on daily life, hobbies, family, etc. ✔️ Example: “My Favorite Hobby” (5-6 sentences).

✅ Common Mistakes & Corrections

❌ "i am student" → ✅ "I am a student." ❌ "he go to school." → ✅ "He goes to school."

🟡 2. Intermediate Writing Skills (Fluency & Structure)

✅ Expanding Sentences & Ideas

✔️ Writing detailed sentences using adjectives and adverbs. ✔️ Example: Instead of “The cat ran.”, write “The small black cat ran quickly across the road.”

✅ Paragraph Structure (Topic Sentence, Supporting Details, Conclusion)

✔️ Writing short stories, opinions, and simple reports. ✔️ Example: “A Trip to the Beach” (8-10 sentences).

✅ Email Writing (Formal & Informal)

✔️ Formal: Sending emails for work or school. ✔️ Informal: Writing to a friend or family member.

✅ Commonly Confused Words & Homophones

✔️ Their / There / They’re – "They left their bags over there." ✔️ Your / You’re – "You’re doing great with your writing!"

🔴 3. Advanced Writing Skills (Professional & Creative Writing)

✅ Essay Writing (Introduction, Body, Conclusion)

✔️ Opinion essays – "Is social media good or bad?" ✔️ Argumentative essays – "Should students wear uniforms?"

✅ Storytelling & Creative Writing

✔️ How to write short stories and dialogues. ✔️ Example: "An Unexpected Adventure."

✅ Business Writing (Reports, Memos, Proposals)

✔️ Writing professional documents for companies. ✔️ Example: Job application letters & business emails.

✅ Academic Writing (Summaries, Research Reports)

✔️ Writing structured academic papers for school or university. ✔️ Example: "The Effects of Climate Change."

✅ Proofreading & Editing

✔️ Checking for spelling, grammar, and clarity. ✔️ How to edit and improve writing step by step.

🔥 Bonus Features

✅ Daily Writing Practice – Small writing exercises for improvement.

✅ Writing Competitions & Challenges – Fun activities to boost confidence.

✅ Grammar & Vocabulary Enhancement – Use better words in writing.
