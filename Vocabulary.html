📚 English Vocabulary Course

🔹 Course Duration: 1 to 3 Months (Based on Student Level)

🔹 Course Goal:

✅ Learn new words to express thoughts clearly and confidently.

✅ Understand word meanings, synonyms, antonyms, and correct usage.

✅ Improve listening, reading, writing, and speaking skills.

🟢 1. Basic Vocabulary for Beginners

✅ Daily Use Words (200+ Essential Words)

✔️ Greetings: Hello, Good morning, Nice to meet you ✔️ Common Verbs: Eat, Drink, Walk, Run, Talk, Sleep ✔️ Family Words: Father, Mother, Brother, Sister, Uncle, Aunt ✔️ Numbers & Time: One, Two, Three, Morning, Afternoon, Evening ✔️ Common Prepositions: In, On, Under, Behind, Between ✔️ Question Words: What, Where, When, Why, How 🔹 Example Sentences: ✔️ "I wake up at 7 AM." ✔️ "She goes to school every day."

🟡 2. Intermediate Vocabulary for Fluency

✅ Descriptive Words (Adjectives & Adverbs)

✔️ Big, Small, Tall, Short, Fast, Slow, Beautiful, Ugly ✔️ Quickly, Slowly, Carefully, Loudly

✅ Common Synonyms & Antonyms

✔️ Happy – Joyful, Cheerful // Sad – Unhappy, Miserable ✔️ Easy – Simple, Effortless // Difficult – Hard, Complicated

✅ Phrasal Verbs (Common Expressions)

✔️ Give up (Quit) – "He gave up smoking." ✔️ Look after (Take care of) – "She looks after her baby." ✔️ Run out of (Finish) – "We ran out of milk."

✅ Common Idioms & Their Meaning

✔️ "Break a leg!" – Good luck! ✔️ "A piece of cake" – Something very easy ✔️ "Hit the books" – To study hard

🔴 3. Advanced Vocabulary for Mastery

✅ Business & Professional Vocabulary

✔️ Resume, Interview, Deadline, Meeting, Presentation ✔️ Formal vs. Informal Words – (Formal: "I would like to request") vs. (Informal: "I wanna ask")

✅ Advanced Phrasal Verbs & Idioms

✔️ Pull someone's leg – Joke with someone ✔️ Go the extra mile – Do more than expected ✔️ Think outside the box – Be creative

✅ Academic & IELTS Vocabulary

✔️ Common words used in essays, reports, and debates ✔️ How to use formal vocabulary correctly

✅ Conversational Slang & Expressions

✔️ "What's up?" – How are you? ✔️ "I'm down for it!" – I agree! ✔️ "No worries!" – It's okay!

🔥 Bonus Features

✅ Daily Speaking Practice – Use new words in conversations

✅ Vocabulary Quizzes & Flashcards – Fun word games

✅ Role-Plays & Debates – Apply vocabulary in real-life situations
