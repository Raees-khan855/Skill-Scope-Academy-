📚 Spoken English Course Outline

🔹 Course Duration Options

✅ 1-Month Crash Course (Basic) ✅ 2-Month Standard Course (Intermediate) ✅ 3-Month Advanced Course

🔹 Course Levels & Topics

🟢 1. Basic Level (Beginners) – 1 Month

Goal: Build basic vocabulary, pronunciation, and confidence in speaking.

🔹 Week 1: Foundations of English Speaking

✔️ Common greetings & introductions ✔️ Daily used words & sentences ✔️ Basic sentence structure ✔️ Pronunciation exercises

🔹 Week 2: Vocabulary & Sentence Formation

✔️ Common verbs, nouns, and adjectives ✔️ Talking about yourself & your daily routine ✔️ Basic question formation (What, Where, When, Why, How) ✔️ Speaking practice – Introducing yourself

🔹 Week 3: Basic Conversations

✔️ Talking about family, hobbies, and interests ✔️ Making requests & asking for help ✔️ Giving directions (left, right, straight, etc.) ✔️ Common shopping & restaurant conversations

🔹 Week 4: Improving Fluency

✔️ Speaking about past and future events ✔️ Role-playing real-life situations ✔️ Expressing likes, dislikes, and opinions ✔️ Daily practice with a partner

🟡 2. Intermediate Level – 2 Months

Goal: Improve fluency, grammar, and confidence in speaking longer sentences.

🔹 Week 5: Grammar for Speaking

✔️ Present, past, and future tenses in spoken English ✔️ Common mistakes & how to correct them ✔️ How to make longer sentences

🔹 Week 6: Building Confidence in Speaking

✔️ Describing people, places, and objects ✔️ How to tell a short story in English ✔️ Expressing emotions (happy, sad, surprised, etc.) ✔️ Group discussions on general topics

🔹 Week 7: Conversational English

✔️ Making phone calls in English ✔️ Handling customer service conversations ✔️ How to negotiate and persuade ✔️ Common English idioms & phrases

🔹 Week 8: Real-Life Speaking Practice

✔️ Role-playing different scenarios (job interview, traveling, ordering food) ✔️ Speaking in front of a group ✔️ How to think in English instead of translating

🔴 3. Advanced Level – 3 Months

Goal: Master fluency, public speaking, and business English.

🔹 Week 9: Advanced Vocabulary & Expressions

✔️ How to sound natural like a native speaker ✔️ Learning phrasal verbs & slang ✔️ Professional & casual speaking styles

🔹 Week 10: Public Speaking & Debates

✔️ How to give a speech in English ✔️ Debate & discussion skills ✔️ How to convince & influence others in English

🔹 Week 11: Business English & Interviews

✔️ Professional email writing ✔️ Speaking confidently in meetings ✔️ Common job interview questions & answers

🔹 Week 12: Final Review & Certification

✔️ Final assessment (speaking test) ✔️ Tips for continuous improvement ✔️ Graduation & certificates for students

🔹 Special Features of the Course

✅ Daily Speaking Practice – Group discussions, one-on-one practice

✅ Accent & Pronunciation Training – Learn neutral and American/British accents ✅ Live Role-Playing – Practice real-life situations ✅ Confidence Boosting Activities – Public speaking & storytelling ✅ Certificate of Completion – At the end of the course
